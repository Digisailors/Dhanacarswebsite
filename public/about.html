About us



DHANA CARS

We, the champions of car decors, started our venture in Tuticorin on 2nd May 2010 with the aim to gratify the car lovers' having strong liking for car accessories. We have a phenomenal system providing complete and quality accessories for cars. We, provide both external and internal car accessories like Audio systems, Polish, Seat Covers, Stickers, alloy wheels, Remote lock, Body kit, Angel and Xenon Light, Sun Control Films, Bumper Guards, etc...

Our Strength is to maintain Quality cum innovation and give the customers the consistent quality at a price which gives the best value for money and are constantly adapted to meet the need of the customers. We provide you huge stocking capabilities of various types of auto accessories.
